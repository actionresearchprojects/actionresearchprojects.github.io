LinkedIn Integration Setup

1. Create a LinkedIn App

  • Go to developer.linkedin.com/apps
  • Click "Create App"
  • Fill in your company details and link your company LinkedIn page
  • Under "Products", request access to "Share on LinkedIn" and "Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect"

2. Get your credentials

  • In your app's "Auth" tab, note your Client ID and Client Secret
  • You'll need to generate an access token — the GitHub Actions workflow includes a helper for this

3. Add GitHub repo secrets

  • Go to your repo Settings → Secrets → Actions
  • Add: LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN
  • Add: LINKEDIN_ORG_ID (your company page's numeric ID)

4. Finding your Organization ID

  • Go to your LinkedIn company page
  • Click "Admin tools" → the URL will contain your org ID
  • Or: go to the page source and search for organization:XXXXXXX

Once configured, checking "Cross-post to LinkedIn" will automatically post to your company page when you publish.

Insects
2
Insects are crucial pollinators, decomposers, and a food source for many other animals. They are excellent indicators of environmental changes and biodiversity.
3
Note: Some of these animals are specific to certain areas of the United Kingdom. This list aims not to provide a 'catch-them-all' list, but rather list all the animals that indicate biodiversity or directly contribute to an ecosystem.

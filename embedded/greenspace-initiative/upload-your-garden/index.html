Upload your Garden

If you have designed a resilient greenspace of your own, you can use the tool provided below to submit information about your garden to us, providing useful information that will be fed back into our guidance.

To complete the spreadsheet, please enter values in the fields highlighted in blue. We ask for mandatory fields (red text) to be completed and for one single photo to be provided for each given layer, each constructed feature, and each output type. The spreadsheet will provide a checklist to help you with this.

Once you have filled out the spreadsheet, please email it to Archie Worthington at archwrth@gmail.com, and, if you consent, we will include it in our Gallery of Inspiration and use it to improve our educational material.

To send images, please either upload them to a site such as Google Photos or Postimages and provide the link within the designated area of the spreadsheet, or include them in your email to us alongside the spreadsheet.

We look forward to hearing from you!
